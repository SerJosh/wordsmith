Welcome to Wordsmith

A space where you can turn your ideas into words, see them displayed to you on your screen, have the opportunity to manage them as you wish and share them with others around the world!

Wordsmith gives you the opportunity to develop your own short story, and your short story can be anything you desire as long as it only uses words of 50000 characters(letters and spacings). Once you have registered, you will have the opportunity to create your own story and read other peoples stories. You will be asked to give your story a Title, and given the options to give it a Blurb (the back of a book), an image for your story and of course the content. Once you are happy just click on create, if you want anything changed in your story click on edit and if you want to delete your story just click delete. And ofcourse if you want to read your or other stories just click on read.

I leave it up to you and your imagination, Enjoy!
