Welcome to Wordsmith

A space where you can turn your ideas into words, see them displayed to you on your screen, have the opportunity to manage them as you wish and share them with others around the world!

Wordsmith gives you the opportunity to develop your own short story,. Your short story can be anything you desire as long as it only uses words of 50000 characters(letters and spacings). Once you have registered, you will have the opportunity to create your own story and read other peoples stories. In the creation of your story, you will give your story a Title, a Blurb (the back of a book), an image (Cover) for your story and of course the Story (content). Once you are happy just click on create. If you want anything changed in your story click on edit and if you want to delete your story just click delete. And of course if you want to read your or other stories just click on read.

I leave it up to you and your imagination, Enjoy!
